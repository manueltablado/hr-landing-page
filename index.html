    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why is it free?
    We believe in making quality healthcare and wellness guidance accessible to everyone. Our commitment to community health and well-being drives us to offer free consultations. We're dedicated to helping individuals lead healthier lives without financial barriers.
    Do I need this if I’ve had my yearly medical check? What is the difference?
    While your annual medical check is essential, our consultations provide a more comprehensive approach. We go beyond the routine check-up, offering personalized guidance and wellness strategies tailored to your unique health goals. Think of us as your partners in long-term well-being, helping you make the most of your health journey.
    Can I bring another person to the appointment?
    Absolutely! We encourage the participation of friends or family to provide support and understanding during the consultation.
    How long does it take?
    Our consultations are designed to be thorough and informative. The duration typically ranges from 30 minutes to 1 hour. We allocate the necessary time to address your concerns, provide in-depth information, and ensure that you leave with a clear understanding of your health and wellness plan.
    What if I have to cancel or if I’m not sure I’ll be able to make it?
    Life is unpredictable, and we understand that. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please let us know as soon as possible. We're here to accommodate your schedule and make sure you receive the care and guidance you need, even in the face of unexpected changes.
    Do I need to bring anything with me?
    Bring any relevant medical or health records you may have, but don't worry if you don't have any.
    What if I don’t have insurance?
    Our consultations are free and open to everyone, regardless of whether you have health insurance.
    Do the weight loss programs you offer work?
    Yes, our programs are designed to achieve effective results. We tailor the plans to your goals and needs.
    What’s the catch?
    There is no catch. We offer guidance and services with a focus on your well-being.
    Will I have to exhaust myself in the gym to make it work?
    You don't need to overexert yourself at the gym. We tailor training programs to be effective and suitable for your fitness level.
    Where are you located?
    Our consultation is located in [location], but we also offer online consultations for your convenience.
